C64 Colour changer

Load, drag and drop or paste a PNG image and you can pick a colour and replace it with another one. Click "save" to save the new image as "C64 recoloured.png" or save/copy directly on the image. This was created as I was too stupid to understand the recolour feature of multipaint.

This is open source and you can download and contribute on GitHub.

Idea and code by Chris 'Cupid' Heilmann (@codepo8)
